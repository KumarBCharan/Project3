*ABOUT MTDC*

Mother Theresa Degree College is located in lush green environment, the College has witnessed a steady growth from 60 to more than 1500 students and is proud of its diverse student body, faculty and staff. Mother Theresa Degree College was established in the academic year 2001-2002. This has been affiliated to Sri Venkateswara University, Tirupathi, Andhra Pradesh.
